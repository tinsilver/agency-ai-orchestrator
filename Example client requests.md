# Example client requests

## Example 1

client: [luckyjumperfilms.co.uk]

request: Original Request

I would like all aspects of the site updated to optimise SEO naturally. Link it to social media accounts like YouTube, LinkedIn and Instagram. Help and advise with writing copy and FAQ section.

Classification

Category: seo_optimization
Subcategories: content_update, feature_request

Missing Information

The following details are needed before this can be turned into a technical spec:

Which specific pages should be prioritized for SEO optimization (all pages or specific ones)?
What are your target keywords or key phrases for the business?
Which social media accounts do you have active (provide URLs/handles for YouTube, LinkedIn, Instagram)?
Do you have existing FAQ content or topics you'd like covered?
What is your target audience and geographic focus (currently mentions West London)?
Do you want the FAQ as a dedicated page or integrated into existing pages?
What tone/style should the copy follow (current tone is professional and emotive)?
Are there specific competitor sites or reference examples for the copy style you prefer?
Do you want schema markup implementation (structured data for SEO)?
What is your timeline for these updates?
